About Us

The IIPS Alumni Association exists to help you stay connected to IIPS, and to each other, whenever you are in your life now. Whatever you're seeking, be it IIPS events and connections in your hometown, an excuse to come back to the Farm for reunion, a job lead or a mind-expanding magazine, we've got you covered.

Our Mission

The IIPS Alumni Association seeks to reach, serve and engage all IIPS alumni and students; to foster a lifelong intellectual and emotional connection between the collage and its graduates; and to provide the University with goodwill and support.

Our History

Founded in 1994 by the University's first graduates, the IIPS Alumni Association aims to deliver the most effective alumni relations program anywhere in higher education. Its diverse offerings online communication and networking tools; academic and social programming (on and off-campus); a worldwide travel/study program; and numerous Stanford-related products and initiatives.
